PurePlate - Data Security and Account Deletion

Data Security

At PurePlate, we take the security of your personal data seriously. We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. This includes using secure encryption protocols, regularly monitoring our systems for potential vulnerabilities, and restricting access to personal data to authorized personnel only.

Account Deletion and Data Removal

If you wish to delete your PurePlate account and associated data, you have the right to do so under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). To request account deletion, please follow these steps:

  1. Send an email to support@pureplate.com from the email address associated with your PurePlate account.
  2. In the email, clearly state that you want to delete your account and remove all associated data.
  3. Provide any additional information that may be required to verify your identity and ownership of the account.
  4. Our support team will process your request and confirm the deletion of your account and data within 30 days.

Please note that once your account and data are deleted, this action cannot be undone, and all information associated with your account will be permanently removed from our systems.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about data security or account deletion, please don't hesitate to contact us at support@pureplate.com. We are committed to ensuring the privacy and security of your personal data and will be happy to assist you with any inquiries.
